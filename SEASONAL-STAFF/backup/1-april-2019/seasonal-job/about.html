About Seasonal Staff NZ

We are a website that only focus on Seasonal and Short term employment jobs. We have created an online platform to make it easier for Seasonal workers and Seasonal Business to connect. We have all heard that “We don’t have enough Seasonal workers”. Well Seasonal Staff NZ is not just another “Jobs board”. Our website has been designed with new way of thinking… it’s been designed For the Seasonal workers by Seasonal workers.

Then we use innovative methods to target the niche market of workers that are Prepared to do seasonal or short term employment. This benefits seasonal Business’s By providing a platform where filling staff vacancies is Quick, easy and cost effective.

Find staff

Easily find staff to fill your short term and seasonal staffing vacancies. Forecast, Plan and advertise the whole year’s vacancies in advance, Search for people who might be Close by who can fill your vacancy now. Know that you are connected to motivated people Who want to do seasonal work, giving you certainty.

Find work

Find Short term and seasonal work by location and month, Plan your working Holiday or short term work, giving you certainty of your ability to earn money. Easily see what other benefits are available …like accommodation, power, water And Wi-Fi or other options like finding a pet friendly work place. Connect with a Community of likeminded people.
